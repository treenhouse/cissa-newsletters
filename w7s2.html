CISSA Peer Mentoring

CISSA Peer Mentoring Program: Calling All Students studying Tech - We’ve Got Something Just for You! 

Audience: Anyone in UniMelb studying Tech related fields (Computer Science, Sofatware Engineering, Data Science, Information Systems)

After last year's success, we are bring the beloved Peer Mentoring Program back again  This is a dedicated opportunity to learn from your peers, and grow together!


For Mentees (First Year & Second Year) : Do you have questions about uni life, study, and career? Want to gain insights from those who’ve been in your shoes? Here’s your opportunity to connect with experienced mentors who are ready to share their wisdom and help you thrive! 


For Mentors (Second Year, Third Year and above - incl. Master students) : Are you passionate about guiding others, passing on the values and knowledge you've received, and helping others succeed? This is your chance to be a mentor and make a real difference in someone’s journey through Computer/Data Science and Information Systems! 


Whether you’re here to guide or to learn, CISSA's Peer Mentoring Program is the place to be! With comprehensive guides provided by CISSA, you’ll have all the support you need to make the most of this experience.
