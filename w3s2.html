Applications are now open for IMC’s Launchpad Program, which takes place from Wednesday, 2 October, to Friday, 4 October.

Join Launchpad, an exclusive 3-day program for forward-thinking students graduating in 2026 and beyond. This immersive experience offers a unique opportunity to accelerate your career and gain an insight into the lives of our traders and engineers.

Put theory into practice by developing your own trading strategy using our cutting-edge trading simulation, and advance your coding skills in our tailored software and hardware workshops. You’ll also get the chance to get to know the IMC team during desk shadowing and networking sessions. Stand-out performers will be fast-tracked to the final round of interviews for the 2025/26 Summer Internship Program in Trading, Software, or Hardware.

IMC’s Launchpad Program is a unique opportunity to apply maths and problem-solving skills in trading and engineering. Exclusively for students graduating in 2026 and beyond, this 3-day immersive experience offers insights into the exciting world of trading and engineering. Learn more: https://bit.ly/4cENe2P
