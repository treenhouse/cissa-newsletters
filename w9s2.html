As many of you know, the club had it's Annual General Meeting (AGM) to conclude the 2023/24 term, and kick off the 2024/25 term. It was incredible to see such a strong turnout from the team, previous team members and general members there to support the club.

All four constitutional amendments passed, which means Publicity Director has been split into Design Director and Marketing Director, Projects Director has been enshrined, and being an international student is now an additional way to qualify for Diversity Director.

We then heard from the President Cinque, along with our Treasurer Jennifer and Education Director Kevin who reported on the state of the club.

Finally, it is with great pleasure to announce the incoming committee for 2024/2025. A huge congratulations to all, and commiserations to those who did not get the roles they were after. I hope it doesn't discourage you from continuing to volunteer in club land, whether at CISSA or in another shape or form.
