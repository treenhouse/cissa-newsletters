Intro to Generative AI and LLM Workshop

CISSA invites you to a 1.5-hour workshop designed to open the doors of artificial intelligence to curious minds from all backgrounds. 

This session is perfect for those looking to grasp the essentials of LLMs, the sophisticated technology behind ground-breaking AI applications such as ChatGPT!! 


In just 90 minutes, we'll cover the core concepts of LLMs, including their design, how they learn from vast amounts of data , and their ability to understand and generate human-like text. 

Through interactive discussions and examples, you'll see first-hand the impact of LLMs across various sectors, from automating customer service to powering advanced chatbots like ChatGPT. 

This workshop doesn't require any prior knowledge in AI or coding, making it an ideal starting point for students, professionals, and anyone else intrigued by the potential of AI to transform our world.  Join us to demystify LLMs and take the first step into the future of technology!
