Intro to Cloud Computing Workshop

Interested in cloud computing? Join our Introduction to Cloud Computing workshop with a Cloud Solution Architect from Microsoft! 

Discover the transformative power of cloud technology, a key driver of innovation and growth across all industries.  Cloud computing is the backbone of modern technology, enabling advancements like AI-driven large language models, seamless online services, and big data analytics.


Through real-world success stories from tech giants, you'll see first hand how cloud computing catalyses digital transformation. This workshop is not just a learning opportunity; it’s your launchpad into mastering cloud solutions, empowering you to shape the future of technology.  


Date: Wednesday, 21st August

Automotive Embedded Systems Development in C Workshop - Quick Release

Curious about the tech that powers your drive? Our upcoming workshop with Quick Release offers a hands-on introduction to automotive embedded systems development using C.

We'll explore the crucial role of ECUs, why C is the go-to for real-time applications, and how Real-Time Operating Systems keep everything running smoothly. Plus, dive into essential testing methods that ensure your work fits seamlessly into larger projects. 

 

This is your chance to see how industry processes shape the day-to-day tasks of future engineers!!! 


Date: Thursday 22nd August
Time: 5:30PM - 7:30PM
Location: Alan Gilbert 120

Quick Release_

Quick Release_ Australia is the local subsidiary of Alten group in Australia.


A leader in Engineering and Technology Consulting, Alten supports its customers development strategies in the areas of innovation, R&D, and information systems. Quick Release_ is a leading Product Data Management (PDM) consultancy.


In Australia, Quick Release_ employs 50+ consultants based in Melbourne, Sydney, and Brisbane and carries over both Quick Release_ market (PDM services and Engineering Services). We support locally the defence, automotive and rail sector with the ambition to extend our activities beyond.
