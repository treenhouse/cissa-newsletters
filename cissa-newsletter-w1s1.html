DiversiTEA

CISSA is excited to hold our first weekly coffee event, DiversiTEA, for this semester! Join us every Wednesday from 12:30 PM to 1:30 PM at House of Cards along Engineering Lane for free coffee, tea, and good vibes.

 Starting from Week 1, come and unwind with other members of the CISSA community in a cozy and welcoming atmosphere.

Don't forget to sign up and become a member, which is free and easy.

See you there, and let's kick off the semester with a caffeine boost!

Location: House of Cards
