#sponsor_events_and_opportunities

Churchill

Churchill Asset Management is looking to hire postgraduate and PhD students for their well-sought-after graduate and internship programs!
Details for the two positions they are hiring are below:
✨ Fundamental Equities Analyst (Full Time): Graduates under this role will have responsibility over conducting research that will determine the true valuation of innovative tech companies and making recommendations to invest or not to invest. For example, how much is Tesla Inc. worth as a company? The key requirement is a strong interest in understanding the true value of technologies, and a strong passion for investing in tech companies particularly those listed in the United States stock exchanges.
✨ Quantitative Investment Analyst (Full Time): Graduates under this role will have responsibility for creating quantitative investment strategies that take into account behaviours of investor sentiment to systematically reallocate money or capital from overhyped stocks to underhyped stocks to simultaneously maximise return and minimise risk. This role requires a mix of three key skills including Python, Mathematics, and Passion for Macroeconomics/Investing.
Apply now at https://lnkd.in/gVsCF4MP 💻
