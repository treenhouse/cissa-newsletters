CIS Student Festival

Includes the CIS Academic Advising Expert Panel & Industry Connect

You’re invited to attend two incredible events on March 13 as part of the CIS Student Festival!

 

The inaugural CIS Student Festival aims to showcase, support and amplify the valuable offerings available to our students.  

 

Kicking this off will be the CIS Academic Advising Expert Panel, a free event for all students to come and hear more about our subjects and possible career pathways from some of our amazing academics and industry partners.

 

Our sensational panel includes Prof Eduard Hovy (primary interest: Artificial Intelligence), A/Prof Toby Murray (primary interest: Computer Security) and A/Prof Giuseppe Barca (primary interest: High-Performance Computing).

 

As an extra special giveaway, the first 100 students arriving at the event will also receive a free gift!

 

Following the panel is the CISSA Industry Connect event, a more informal setting for students to network with academics and industry leaders one on one to discuss study and career opportunities.

 

Register now so you don’t miss out on the chance to personally speak to people who have been where you are and can provide useful and practical advice!


Date & Time: Wednesday 5pm - 8:30pm

Location: The Forum, Melbourne Connect

IMC Trading


Applications for our exclusive Internship and Women in Trading & Engineering programs are now open! This is your chance to experience life at IMC, learn about what we do and have a chance to land your dream job.


Women in Trading & Engineering program, this is what you’ll get:

  • Explore and understand Engineering and Trading at IMC through our workshops and simulations.

  • Get to know current IMC employees during networking sessions and desk shadowing.

  • Develop highly sought-after skills to set you up for success in the industry.

  • Immerse yourself in IMC’s social life and events.


Internship program, this is what you’ll get:

  • An industry-competitive remuneration package.

  • A 10-week Internship program where you will be immersed in real projects with complex problem solving.

  • Have opportunities to learn from some of our industry’s strongest Traders and Engineers.

  • In-house chef and barista providing you breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks daily.

  • Have support from a mentor who will oversee your progress and professional development.

  • The opportunity to secure a full-time role with IMC once you graduate.
