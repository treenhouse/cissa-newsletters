Coming Up Week 10

FinTech 101: Exploring the World of Airwallex

Join us for an exclusive deep dive into the exciting world of FinTech  with Jonathan Latti and David Fletcher, Software Engineers from our industry partner Airwallex! This event is a must for any IT student ready to kickstart their career in software engineering, especially in the booming FinTech industry .


Airwallex, a global leader in revolutionizing cross-border payments , gives software engineers the chance to work with cutting-edge technologies  and shape the future of the industry. Jonathan and David will dive into the FinTech space, share insights on their work, and give you a glimpse into the real-world problems they solve daily at Airwallex .

Plus, don’t miss Greg Thom, Early Careers Program Manager at Airwallex, who will reveal the top qualities they look for in software engineers  and give you pro tips on how to land a role at this innovative company .

Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to learn from industry experts and gain a competitive edge in your future career!
