Codebrew!

Mark April 4-7 on your calendar for a once-in-a-lifetime experience:

72 hours of HACKING filled with inspiration, creativity, teamwork and LOTS of Redbull + Coffee!

For the first time, participants will be able to stay at the event venue overnight! Codebrew is open to university students of ALL SKILL LEVELS!

First timer? Fret not! We’ll have plenty of educational workshops leading to and on the day of the hackathon and many brilliant mentors on the day!

Team up with a few mates or find like-minded people at our in-person team formation session / on our Discord server, and venture into the world of hacking together!


WHAT’S IN IT FOR ME?

  • Workshops throughout the week prior & Codebrew weekend! Topics include: Front End, Backend, Git, tech stacks, UI/UX design, pitching etc, taught by our education team.
     thousands of dollars worth of free food!

  • Prize pool, with 9 categories totalling to over $3400+

  • Raffle draws throughout Codebrew weekend even if you're just hacking to have fun and not to win!

  • Mentors who regularly check in and answer your questions!

  • Free merch including
    - CISSA stickers and keep cups
    - Sponsor merch

  • Social night to help you recover from hacking and make new friends in the tech community!

Atlassian

A reminder that Atlassian’s various internship positions are still open! Applications close on 12th May, so make sure you get your applications in as soon as possible! We hire on a rolling basis, so the roles could potentially be filled before the close date. Don't delay in applying!


The eligibility criteria for internships are below:

  • Must be in the penultimate year of a full-time Bachelor’s or Master’s degree (finishing your studies in mid or end of 2025)

  • Have less than 6 months of full-time professional working experience, not including internship, co-op, or during-degree consulting work experiences

  • Applications will only be accepted from Australian and New Zealand Citizens, and Permanent Residents

  • Must be available to work full-time for 12 weeks between late November 2024 and early February 2025


We are currently taking applications for the following internships:

  • Software Engineering

  • Associate Product Management

  • Content Design

  • Product Design

  • Financial Planning and Analysis

  • Accounting Specialist
